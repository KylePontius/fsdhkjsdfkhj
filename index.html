What is an Algorithmic Trader?

An algorithmic trader is someone who creates an automated program to execute trades in a methodical manner, with the intent to gain gain profits. The education level needed for this job typically requires a bachelor's degree in CS, with experience in finance. Due to this being a more technical heavy job, sole experience in computer science could be applicable. The payout for this job is heavily dependent on performance where one is employed.
